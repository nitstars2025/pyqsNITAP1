Our Objective

Our objective is to make academic preparation easier and more effective for every student of NIT Arunachal Pradesh. Through this platform, we aim to provide quick and organized access to past year question papers of B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs. By creating a reliable academic resource built by students, for students, we hope to encourage a culture of knowledge-sharing and collaboration. We are committed to continuously improving this platform with valuable feedback, ensuring that it benefits not only the present batches but also future generations of learners.
